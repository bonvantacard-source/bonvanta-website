FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do customers need to download an app?

No. Customers open your QR link and save the loyalty card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet. No separate Bonvanta app is required.

What is a card operation?

A card operation is an activity processed for a loyalty card, such as issuing or updating a card. The dashboard shows your usage for the current period.

What happens after the 14-day Growth trial?

No payment is taken automatically. Unless you choose a paid plan, the account returns to Free Forever and remains subject to the Free plan limits.

Can I cancel a paid plan?

Yes. You can cancel at any time. Access continues until the end of the paid billing period, subject to the Terms and Refund Policy.

Can I use Bonvanta outside the United States?

Yes. Bonvanta is available worldwide, subject to applicable local laws, wallet availability, and payment support.
